Basetrack Disclaimer

Basetrack is an independent, civilian media project, funded by a 2010 Newschallenge Grant from the John S. and James L. Knight Foundation. It is not owned, or operated by the U.S. Marine Corps or the U.S. Department of Defense. Opinions expressed here are attributed to individuals and do not represent official statements from any of the above institutions.

Family members of 1/8 Marines seeking official points of contact for 1st Battalion, 8th Marines, should refer to the Family Readiness webpage.

Following the current 1/8 Deployment to Afghanistan, Basetrack will continue to operate as an open-source media system, designed to offer a free publication platform for other independent reporting projects.
