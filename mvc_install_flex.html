The Road to Huruma is a desktop application that functions as a gateway to Media Voices content.

By installing it on your computer, you open the door to accessing the latest in videos, documents and photography assembled on the Media Voices site.

The application talks directly to our servers to present the latest material we have posted. But because this is a desktop application, its moveable - the application can run from your computer, be burned to a CD or DVD or copied onto a USB key.

What this means is twofold: you can distribute this "gateway" to content to friends and colleagues and also use it as point of entry to original content we are creating and distributing from Media Voices.

The application automatically reads the latest material from the site and also notifies you when an update is available. Once installed on your desktop, its yours to view, distribute and use as a reference.

Click on the Install Now button at the left to begin the installation process.
